Welcome to the ESO stats page!

This webpage is not affiliated with Microsoft Games, Ensemble Studios, or ESO.

This is a php utility that pulls XML data from the ESO servers and turns it into readable detailed stats.

Features:
- Full AoM:TT support
- Detail Player Stats
- Player Search
- Top 1000 Players Real time (grouped into 10 pages)
- Recent Game Lists
- Full GameStats with Graphs
- Detailed Population Statistics
- Time Offset setting with cookies
